Howdy!
The Texas A&M Wesley Foundation is a home away from home for the students of Texas A&M University and Blinn College in the Bryan-College Station area. We are a community dedicated to growing closer to God and one another through worship, fellowship, service, and study.
Worship
We have dinner and worship every Sunday and Wednesday evening, allowing us to move from a time of food and fellowship directly into a time of worship. Dinner begins at 6:00 PM and worship begins at 7:00 PM both days.
