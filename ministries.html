Sunday Night Worship
We come together every Sunday night for a time of fellowship and worship, starting with dinner, which is provided by one of the Sunday School classes from a local church, at 6:00 PM and moving into a time of worship at 7:00 PM. Our Sunday worship services are a blend of traditional and contemporary styles, with a band playing songs of praise as well as a pianist leading us in congregational hymns. The sermon is delivered by our campus minister, Max Mertz, over topics that are relevant and challenging to a community of college students. After worship ends, we move back into fellowship with a time of milk and cookies.
Wednesday Night Worship
Wednesday nights are structured much like Sundays, with dinner at 6:00 PM and worship at 7:00 PM, but the evening is led even more completely by students. Students cook the meal, students lead worship, and we even have a student deliver the message! Our praise band on Wednesdays plays in a more rock-influenced style, employing electric instruments where the Sunday band uses things like accordion or violin. As mentioned, the sermon is delivered by a student leader from within our community, allowing them to speak on topics that are important for their friends and peers to hear. Like Sundays, we have milk and cookies provided by students after worship ends.
